MEET THE CHEF

Chef Élise Moreau

Executive Chef & Culinary Director

With over 18 years of experience across Michelin-starred kitchens in Paris, Lyon, and Tokyo, Chef Élise brings a philosophy of disciplined simplicity to Maison Noir. Her menus change with the seasons, always honoring local produce while drawing on classical French technique.

Trained under legendary chefs Paul Bocuse and Anne-Sophie Pic, Élise has earned a reputation for menus that feel both daring and deeply comforting.
